About me

Hey! I'm Jack, and I work as a data analyst but have a background in microbiology. I started acquiring an interest in data and programming as a student, when I realised the importance that it plays in informing many aspects of research. I found it fascinating how people such as bioinformaticians process and organise biological data using programming, and the impact that they have had in the world of genetics. After a few years working in laboratories, I decided that I wanted to focus more on developing data skills and in 2022 I took the plunge and managed to successfully break into the industry as an analyst at Tesco after self-learning SQL and python. At Tesco I had the opportunity to further develop my SQL skills, specifically in SQL Server. I also picked up Tableau, a BI tool which I shortly became certified in and discovered that I have a penchant for building dashboards. In 2023 I joined People's Postcode Lottery (PPL), again as an analyst, searching for further opportunities to continue my career development. At PPL I've continued to develop both my SQL and python skills by exploring my interests in data engineering, working on automation and pursuing data engineering topics, alongside self-directed studying. My long-term aspiration is to be a data engineer and I am totally committed to the journey!
