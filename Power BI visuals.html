Global company sales

My first ever Power BI visual, with the scenario based on fictional data regarding global company sales. I used DAX functions to create yearly breakdowns and added measures to work out total and average sales. Shown are various summary visuals with slicers for filtering. Navigation buttons are included to the different pages of the report for a more in-depth look.
