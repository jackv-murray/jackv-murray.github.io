A humble microbiologist's journey to data science-hood

Hello! My name is Jack and I'm a data analyst. I originally graduated in 2019 with a BSc. in microbiology and worked in laboratories in both a quality control and research environment - hence the bug to (de)bug! I still love science but became interested in insight and data analytics and started my data journey by learning SQL and python. Now I write queries and build dashboards, producing actionable insight for stakeholders in a business setting. I still maintain my interest in learning and self-development; right now I am currently exploring more advanced statistics and machine learning topics.

This is a showcase of my personal projects and abilities, including python, SQL and Power BI - I hope you enjoy!
